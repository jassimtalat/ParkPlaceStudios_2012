Services

Training:

Park Place Studio provides training for new installations including Lighting Direction, Camera Placement and Shot Making and Floor direction for news casts, Interview and sports shows. We can create flow charts for production and consultations for work process and broadcast product production. We also design sets for Live productions and coordinate the building, video and lighting for these projects.

  • HDTV Set Design for News Weather and Sports and Information
  • Content and New Media Broadcast Presentation Design
  • Sports Center and sport specific set design
  • HDTV transition consultation and planning
  • Weather Center and Weather presentation area design
  • Consultation for newsroom design and studio architecture
  • Set Design for Robotic camera systems and automated remote broadcasts
  • Art direction for specialty, information and entertainment presentations
  • Project management including construction documents, construction management, installation, detailing and lighting direction
  • Training and complete Project Documentation
