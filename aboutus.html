About Us

Park Place Studio is a set design company. For more than 20 years, Park Place has created dynamic and unique visual environments for news and information broadcasts. President Park Warne who leads the company, has an extensive background in art direction, stage design and broadcast presentation solutions. The company has become, and has remained, one of the top set design companies in the world. Park Place understands the current evolution of broadcast and has been leading of the development of the Content Distrubution design to incorporate new media and technology.
