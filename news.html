Exhibits April 16 – 19

Las Vegas Convention Center, Las Vegas, Nevada USA

Join us at the NAB 2012. We will be exhibiting with the Vitec group in Booth # SL5915. We will be demonstrating our Set Design capabilities and offering seminars on the how the latest in Display and Lite Panels LED technology have created revolutionary changes in Boradcast Presentation Environments.

We will also be offering seminars ,with our partner URS, on how Digital Architecture, Space Design and Content Distribution have changed the presentation and engineering dynamic for studio and newsroom design, news presentation and information distribution.

We will be offering tours of KTNV, Ch 13 in Las Vegas to demonstrate how our design ideas have been created, presented and implemented for local news. KTNV is on the air 10 hours a day with news and local programming. See for your self how we identified and created solutions their particular broadcast demands, on time and on budget.
